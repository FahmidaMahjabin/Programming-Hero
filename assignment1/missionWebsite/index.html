Stay Focused on Mission 2022

The goals of software engineering are straightforward and easy to understand – but they aren’t always easy to meet. This is because there are so many different ways to approach software engineering and so many outcomes that are possible. While we do have best practices and there are standards in place, every software engineer has a different approach and sometimes they don’t always mesh well with other members of an IT team. So our mission is to become a successful software engineer.
